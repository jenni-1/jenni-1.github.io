Podcast Recommendations

Past and present favorites that would be appropriate for young kiddos.

Links are all to Apple Podcasts (you might have to push and hold to open the link), but I'm sure you can find most of them on Spotify, Amazon Music, etc. I personally use the Overcast app.

All ages:

Note: Some are might be better for 5 year olds, depending on the episode - the science and tech ones might sometimes be over their head.

  • Circle Round - Stories
  • Aaron’s World - Created by a 6 year old and all about dinosaurs
  • The Cramazingly Incredifun Sugarcrash Kids Podcast - Stories and more
  • Little Stories for Tiny People
  • Stories Podcast
  • Story Pirates - Personal favorite - it's silly and entertaining for adults as well
  • Story Time - Look up the “Dog King” episodes, personal favorites
  • Story Nory
  • Wow in the World - Science and tech
  • But Why? A Podcast for Curious Kids - Self explanatory. :) They answer kid’s questions about all sorts of stuff
  • Bedtime History - History - Sonja’s fav
  • Brains On! - Science
  • Smash Boom Best - A fun “debate” show with kid judges
  • What If World - Fun stories inspired by kids' imaginations

For older kids:

  • Eleanor Amplified
  • Mars Patel (can listen on their website for free)
  • The Two Princes - Only on Spotify
  • Grimm, Grimmer, Grimmest (From Pinna, requires subscription for some content)
  • Goodnight Stories for Rebel Girls

Others I just looked up that sound great:

  • Camp WeWow
  • Ear Snacks
  • Molly of Denali
  • Noodle Loaf (music education)
  • Be Calm on Ahway Island (mindfulness/sleep meditation)
  • The Past and the Curious
  • Planet Storytime

You’ll have to experiment and see which ones your kids gravitate toward. A lot of the story podcasts are hit-or-miss depending on the episode. As you can see, there’s so much good stuff out there. I'm sure I'm missing some good ones.
